about me

I was fortunate to grow up working in my family's business, Guadalajara Mexican Restaurant. Those early years taught me the importance of hard work, the value of a dollar, and provided me with a comprehensive understanding of various aspects of running a business. However, I have recently embarked on a new journey that has proven to be the most challenging yet exciting endeavor: learning how to code. The world of coding has presented me with numerous obstacles, but I firmly believe that consistency is the key to success. Just as I have approached every aspect of my life with unwavering dedication, I have applied the same principle to my coding journey. It hasn't been easy, but I've persevered, constantly pushing myself to learn and grow. My goal in this field is clear: to reach the finish line someday. I am determined to become proficient in coding, mastering the skills and knowledge required to excel in this dynamic and ever-evolving industry. Through persistence, hard work, and a commitment to continuous learning, I am confident that I will achieve my objectives and make a meaningful impact in the world of technology.
