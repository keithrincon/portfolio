about me

After completing many web development courses and web apps my next step is to land a web development position. Despite growing up in my family’s successful restaurant starting at a young age it has taught me many valuable lessons, skills and business practices. Therefore, you can expect me to carry an entire project or even the business on my shoulders and treat it as if it were mine because that’s how I’ve always worked.

My goal is to get into the tech industry by this year. Upon completing my most recent certifications all on my own I’ve been able to build a solid stepping stone that will allow me to start building medium to large web apps. Being self-taught gives me the full confidence that when the time comes of joining a proper team, that the sky’s going to be the limit! I am ready to take what I have learned and start applying it onto real-world projects.
